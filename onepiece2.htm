'One Piece' chapter 886 release date news, spoilers: Gear 4 not enough to defeat Katakuri? Sanji tries to stop Big Mom's tantrums with cake

Juan Miguel Salonga Wed 22 Nov 2017 12:19 GMT

The Whole Cake Island arc continues to unfold in "One Piece," although there are hints that the intense showdown between the Straw Hat pirates, led by Luffy, and the Big Mom pirates, will come to a close. In chapter 886, the fans are likely to witness another heated battle between Luffy and Katakuri, while Sanji and Pudding will serve a sweet delight that may finally end the onslaught of Big Mom.

According to Inquisitr, "One Piece" chapter 886 will show the Straw Hat pirates doing their best to end the conflict with Big Mom. To recap the previous chapter, Luffy was successful in his attempt to escape from the Mirror World, a dimension created by the Big Mom pirates to trap their enemies. With the help of Brulee, Luffy is now in the real world to meet his crew. However, the pirate still has Big Mom and her powerful lieutenants to deal with.

Meanwhile, Sanji, Pudding, and Chiffon are finally ready to serve the cake to Big Mom. In the previous chapters, Sanji learned that there is a possibility to stop Big Mom from pursuing the Straw Hat pirates by giving her a taste of a delicious cake. Sanji is hopeful that if Big Mom likes the cake, she will stop her tantrums and regain her sanity.

Some fans thought that "One Piece" chapter 886 will finally give the resolution to the Whole Cake Island arc of the manga series. It will not be surprising if the Big Mom saga will be over soon, especially if the female Yonkou does like the taste of Sanji's cake and stops throwing tantrums. On the other hand, Luffy will need to finish off Katakuri once and for all, although his Gear 4 technique may not be enough to defeat his opponent. In the next chapter, Luffy will find a way to restore his haki powers within a limited period before he can proceed into fighting Katakuri.

There are other speculations that Luffy will meet another trouble as Big Mom tries to pin him down with a heavy object. The good news is that Big Mom is not in her right mind, and Luffy can easily dodge the objects being thrown his way.

The "One Piece" manga creator, Eiichiro Oda, has taken a one week hiatus before coming up with a new issue. Chapter 886 is expected to be out on Nov. 27.
